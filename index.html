I'm Shifa Naaz — a Computer Vision and ML engineer with a Bachelor's in Computer Science (AI/ML specialization) and a Master's in IT Administration & Management with a Cybersecurity leadership focus at Central Washington University, graduating soon.

I don't just build AI systems — I think about how to protect them. My background spans graduate research in intrusion detection at CWU, research at the Administrative Staff College of India, innovation engineering at KG Reddy's Innovation Cell, and real-time ML projects in medical imaging and audio AI.

Beyond technical work, I've trained 100+ students in coding across 6 schools in India, led cross-cultural programs as an AFS Intern for nearly 3 years, and mentored 40–50 students in ML and NLP fundamentals. I bring strong communication and leadership skills alongside my technical toolkit.

My goal is to bridge AI/ML and cybersecurity — helping organizations build systems that are not just intelligent, but reliable, secure, and responsible.
